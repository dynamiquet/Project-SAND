About Us

Welcome to Project S.A.N.D. (Spreading Awareness of Natural Disasters)! Our mission is to empower communities across the United States with reliable data and insights on natural disasters so that you can be better prepared when emergencies occur.

Our Mission

At Project S.A.N.D., we believe that knowledge is power. By providing county-level hazard ratings and detailed risk data collected by FEMA, we help you understand the natural disasters that could impact your area and guide you to vital preparedness resources.

About the Data

Our website leverages comprehensive data from the FEMA National Risk Index. The dataset includes detailed information on natural disaster frequency, location, type, and risk ratings—ensuring that you get the most reliable and actionable insights.

How to Use Our Website

Use the search bar to look up specific US counties and view hazard ratings for various disasters, or check the “Top 5” page to see the most hazardous risks in your area. Our navigation menu makes it easy to jump to resources, get your current location, or return home whenever needed.

Our Team

We are a dedicated group of developers and disaster preparedness advocates committed to making vital information accessible and actionable. Together, we work tirelessly to help communities stay informed and safe.
