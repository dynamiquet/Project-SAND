Risk Analysis

Our natural hazard risk ratings are calculated by multiplying the likelihood of a natural hazard event ocurring with the consequences of the natural hazard event on the area.

Risk = Likelihood x Consequences

Our rating categories range from "Very Low" to "Very High"
